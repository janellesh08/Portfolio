History

The story of the small town girl is one that I'm sure you've heard many times so one more time can't hurt. I was born and raised in Montgomery, AL. I started working in customer service roles at 18 and since then I worked my way up to dining room manager of a top Atlanta country club. I found that I was ready for a more exciting, stable, and secure career so I decided to make the switch to web development in 2019. I started by teaching myself using any and all free online resources and then jumped into different cohorts and courses to expand on my knowlege. Finally, I found my way to DigitalCrafts Web Development Bootcamp and fully committed to the process of becoming a proficient, confident, and passionate computer programmer. I am excited everyday to see what knew technology I can learn and what creative techniques I can develop.

I attended Auburn University at Montgomery and Troy University but it took me a while to discover what I was really interested in making my career. I had many different jobs over the years ranging from server to kid's club attendant at Gold's Gym. I even joined the Air Force in an attempt to find my forever thing. I realized pretty quickly that none of that was for me but I continued to do it until I decided that I wanted something new. All of my experiences over the years have shaped me into who I am today and I am grateful for each one but I have never been more ready to break into this new chapter.

Today

I love finding projects to work on and improve my skill, however I have a wide variety of interests. I love music, movies, and art in all forms. I rollerskate, paint, make Youtube videos, make natural hair care products and coach cheerleading! If you LOVE my Yzma costume (featured above) I have tons more since Halloween is the most wonderful time of year. My life is organized chaos but always an adventure and I can't wait to see where I go from here. I have a lot of ideas that I can't wait for the world to see!

It's been a pleasure!

Thanks for taking the time to have look into my world. I look forward to working together in the future.
